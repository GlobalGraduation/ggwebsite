Required Documents (Counselling)

We need the following documents and information to apply for the university after student counciling.

  • Academic mark sheets *
    10th, 12th, Under Graduation (if applicable, consolidated memo and provisional certificate/graduation certificate)
  • Statement of purpose (SOP) *
    with plagiarism report (shouldn't exeed 20%)
  • 2 Reference Letters *
    From previous educational institution and company if applicable
  • English language certificate (IELTS, TOEFL, PTE etc.. some Universities are providing an offer based on your academics english scores.)
  • Work Experience letters (if applicable)
  • Passport (Front and Back) *
  • CV *
  • Filled in personal details form *
    all the mandatory fileds should be filled in.
  • University applying *
    go to contact us page for list of universities
  • Course Name and Link *
    find the course in the university website and send the links.
  • Intake *
    you can find the intakes in the corresponding universities websites.

Applying for University

  • We apply to the selected universites and you will receive an acknowledgement from the corresponding university.
  • A conditional/unconditional offer letter will be issued from the university
    If the letter is conditional then we need to fulfill the conditions to get unconditional offer letter(eg of conditions : English score, clear SOP etc.. )
  • Accept your unconditional offer letter
  • Pay the required tuition fee and send us a copy of your TT receipt.
  • This is the time to think about the funds maintenance. (check the next section for details..)
  • Complete your TB Medical Test
  • Please contact us as soon as you get any emails/calls from the university.

Visa Process

  • CAS : Receive CAS (Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies) letter, complete and return financial documents.
