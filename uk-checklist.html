Documents for UK University Admission

No Document
1 SSC/10th Certificate
2 HSC/12th/Intermediate Certificate
3 i) Original Degree Certificate if not available Provisional Certificate PC is must
ii) CMM (if available)
iii) All years/SEMs marks memos
iv) Transcripts (if available)
4 English Certificate (IELTS/TOEFL/DUOLINGO/PTE/GRE/GMAT/SAT)
5 MOI (Medium of Instruction) as English
6 2 LORs (1 from previous college/Uni, 1 from the employer (if not possible then from a colleague), Note if there is no experience then 2 LORs from previous college)
7 CV (Resume) with all details including Academics and Experience dates (Note : there shouldn't be any gaps)
8 SOP (Statement of purpose, plagiarism should be less than 10%)
9 Passport (Front and Back)
10 Work Experience Letters/Offer letter (If you are working)
11 Marriage Certificate (If applicable)
12 Affidavit if the names are different to passport in any of the documents
13 Visa Refusal Documents (If applicable)
